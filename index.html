Industrial Talk 3 Newsletter

The Industrial Talk 3 was conducted by Dr. Seah Choon Sen discussing about I-Farm, an innovative technology that simplifies online vegetable shopping. The focus is on addressing the shortage of vegetables during the COVID-19 lockdown, where people faced challenges like long queues and social distancing in markets. I-Farm ensures convenient online grocery orders with nearby delivery, emphasizing transparency in the vegetable growing process and freshness. Utilizing smart technology and a capable team, I-Farm cultivates and sells vegetables online, supporting local farmers. This not only benefits the environment but also contributes to ensuring sufficient food and minimizing waste. Exploring I-Farm has motivated me to explore similar technological concepts and potentially be involved in such initiatives in the future.
